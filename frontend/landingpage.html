A Tranquil Oasis in the Heart of the Zoo

This zoo garden offers a peaceful escape from the bustling animal exhibits. With beautifully manicured landscapes, tranquil ponds, and fragrant flower beds, it provides visitors with a serene atmosphere to relax, unwind, and enjoy the natural beauty surrounding them.

A Haven for Feathered Wonders

Immerse yourself in a world of colorful plumage and melodic songs as you wander through lush foliage and intricately designed bird habitats. From majestic parrots and graceful peacocks to tiny hummingbirds and rare exotic species, this garden provides a unique opportunity to observe and appreciate the captivating world of birds up close.

Let's discover the paradise

This zoo garden is designed to stimulate the senses and create a whimsical experience for visitors of all ages. listen to the soothing sounds of trickling water, and marvel at the visually stunning displays of vibrant plants.

Exploring the World of Intelligent Primates

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of primates in the Primate Paradise zoo garden. Walk through lush vegetation and observe a variety of primate species swinging, climbing, and socializing in their spacious enclosures. From playful chimpanzees showcasing their intelligence to agile gibbons displaying their acrobatic skills, this garden provides a window into the fascinating lives of our closest relatives in the animal kingdom.
