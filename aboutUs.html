Hello!

Welcome to our website dedicated to the fascinating world of animals! We are a team who shares a love for computer science and a passion for the animal kingdom.

Our team consists of Stefana, Antonia and Roxana and we all share a common goal: to create a platform that educates and entertains people about the diverse and amazing creatures that share our planet. Through our website, we aim to provide visitors with a comprehensive source of information about animals, their habitats, behaviors, and conservation efforts. We have put together a collection of information and photos that showcase the beauty and complexity of the natural world.

We hope that our website inspires people to learn more about animals and to appreciate the wonder and diversity of the natural world. Thank you for visiting our site, and we hope you enjoy exploring it as much as we enjoyed creating it!
